About Me

Hi and welcome to my website. My name is Danielle Beckley, but most people call me Dani. I am a Software Developer in Chicago Illinois. I graduated in December 2016 from The Ohio State University with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering. If you explore this site, you'll see some of my experience in and knowledge of the CSE world; here I'll tell you a bit more about myself outside the world of programming.

Strive to be uncomfortable. I find this sentiment to be relevant and motivational each day; what better way to learn more about yourself and the world around you than by pushing yourself to explore. I push myself to explore, discover, and create new things (applicable to both programming and the world). I love being outdoors, everything from hiking to laying around at the beach. This summer, I've spend my time laying out and reading at beautiful Lake Michigan as well as exploring so many beautiful places. Below is one of the many beautiful places I visited this summer in Wisconsin called the Lion's Den Gorge Nature Preserve.

I really enjoy working out. I am currently doing Anna Victoria's Fit Body Guide and currently am on week 15! I am super proud not only of the time I've committed to this workout, but also the mental and physical changes. Frequently I will workout during lunch which gives me time to clear my mind and move around during the day. There's no mood a good workout can't fix.

Learning new things is important to me, so I spend a lot of time listening to podcasts and music and watching documentaries as well as movies. Some of my favorites include Winter on Fire, Chapo Trap House, and Economic Update.

As is obvious by the fact that I love exploring, I love having new experiences. One way I often do this is by going to comedy shows and concerts. My favorite venue thus far has been Red Rocks Amphitheatre in Colorado. I've been fortunate enough to see two conerts there: Walk the Moon and John Mayer. Below are some of my photos from Red Rocks.

Last but certainly not least are my family and friends. I've meet some amazing friends at college and I am very close with my sisters. We explore together whether it is by going to restauraunts, concerts, hiking, or somewhere in between. I am blessed to have shared many experiences and made many memories with them.
